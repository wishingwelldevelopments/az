Who Are We?

Azzo Project Services Pty Ltd prides itself on professsional management and service delivery second to none. There is no project too complex. Our motto is "100 over 100" our attitude is ethical and professional.

Mark Azzopardi

Mark has over 35 years’ experience in the construction industry with skills in project management (national and international), client management (national and international) and Passive Fire Management as well as being a qualified plasterer and cabinet maker. Over this time, Mark has overseen projects up to $64 million which included Federal capital works; passive protection projects in high rise office fit out, residential buildings, shopping centres, airports, hospitals, national banking rollouts, retail buildings and hotels.

His previous projects have included New Parliament House in Canberra, Hayman Is Redevelopment, Brisbane Square office tower, Redevelopment of the Australian War Memorial, Gladstone Hospital, Proserpine Hospital, Mackay Mater Hospital, James Cook University in Townsville, National Security Rollout for Suncorp Bank, Mine Administration Buildings in the Bowen Basin, LVMH and Hermes expansion in Asia – Pacific Rim – Latin America, Cairns Domestic Airport $12mil, Cairns Base Hospital Passive Fire system upgrade $2mil, Cairns Navy Base $8mil, Hi-rise unit complexes.

Working with clients, builders and architects, Mark has an interest in Safety and Quality and to deliver projects to programme and within budget; based on a sound Project Management Plan. Mark’s qualifications: QBCC Passive Fire Certifier Diploma of Project Management Achieved RegPM status with the Australian Institute of Project Management in 2007
